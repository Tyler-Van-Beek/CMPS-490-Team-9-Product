FAQs

Can I add photos or videos to my event listing?

Yes, you can enhance your event listing by adding media such as images to make it more appealing.

Do I need an account to create or join an event?

Yes, you must sign up for an account to either create or join events on Lagniappe.

Are you able to edit an event once creating it?

Yes, you can edit event details after creating it by navigating to your events dashboard and selecting "Edit Event".

How do I register for an event?

Browse events and click "Register" on the one you want.

Where can I see my registered events?

Go to your profile to view registered events.
